# Femwell

> Empowering Indian women to take control of their hormonal and metabolic health through accessible, science-based support.

## Overview

**Femwell** is a women's hormonal health platform launching in India, targeting Tier 1 and Tier 2 cities. The platform combines teleconsultations, diagnostics, and lifestyle guidance focused on:

- **Menopause & Perimenopause Support**
- **Hormone Replacement Therapy (HRT)**
- **Medical Weight Loss (GLP-1 medications)**
- **Holistic Hormonal Wellness**

## Vision

Create the first comprehensive women's hormonal health category in India, breaking taboos and providing confidential, personalized care through AI-powered support and trusted medical professionals.
