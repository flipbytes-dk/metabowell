## Executive Summary

### Topic
**GLP-1 Medical Weight Loss Platform - Comprehensive Strategic Planning**

A digital health platform for the Indian market focused on supporting patients through their complete GLP-1 (tirzepatide/semaglutide) weight loss journey. The platform integrates teleconsultations, diagnostics, pharmacy access, dietician support, and ongoing patient care into a single seamless experience. Geographic focus on Tier 1 and Tier 2 cities with a phased launch strategy.

### Session Goals
- Explore overall platform concept and value proposition
- Develop business model and pricing strategy
- Define MVP features and product roadmap
- Identify key partnerships (doctors, labs, pharmacies, dieticians)
- Address regulatory compliance concerns
- Create patient acquisition and retention strategies
- Differentiate from hospital-based programs and potential competitors

### Techniques Used
1. **What If Scenarios** (25 min) - Expansive exploration of success factors and opportunities
2. **Role Playing** (25 min) - Multi-stakeholder perspectives (patients, doctors, dieticians, pharmacists)
3. **SCAMPER Method** (30 min) - Structured feature and business model innovation
4. **Assumption Reversal** (20 min) - Risk identification and mitigation planning
5. **Convergence & Prioritization** (20 min) - MVP scope definition and roadmap

### Total Ideas Generated
**60+ actionable ideas** across platform features, business strategy, partnerships, competitive positioning, and risk mitigation

### Key Themes Identified:
- **Continuous Care vs. Prescribe & Abandon** - Platform fills massive gap in Indian healthcare where patients get prescription but no ongoing support
- **Lifelong Patient Relationships** - Not a weight loss program but a metabolic health companion (patients stay 3-5+ years due to rebound effect)
- **Personal Touch as Moat** - High-touch service that big platforms can't replicate at scale
- **Integration as Value** - Doctors + Labs + Pharmacy + Dieticians + Peer Support in ONE platform
- **Community Creates Retention** - Sense of belonging keeps patients engaged beyond weight loss goals
- **Tier 1 ‚Üí Tier 2 Phasing** - Launch in metros to build awareness, scale to Tier 2 for volume
- **Education-First Approach** - Must convince patients that (1) GLP-1 is safe/effective, (2) obesity is a disease needing treatment
- **Regulatory Research Critical** - Telemedicine prescription regulations could be existential risk

- **Doctor scarcity advantage** - One trusted metro doctor can serve hundreds of Tier 2 patients via telemedicine

- **Cultural insight:** Indian patients rely on doctor's authority, not technology predictions

### Session 4: Assumption Reversal - Reality Check
**Duration:** 20 minutes
**Description:** Challenge core assumptions to identify hidden risks and mitigation strategies

#### Assumption #1: "Doctors will prescribe GLP-1 via telemedicine"

**REVERSAL: What if doctors refuse due to liability/regulatory concerns?**

**Analysis:**
- Could be **existential risk** to business model
- India's Telemedicine Practice Guidelines (2020) have specific rules for Schedule H/H1/X drugs
- GLP-1 classification and prescription requirements unclear
- First consultation requirements may mandate in-person visit
- State-by-state variation possible

**Mitigation Strategies:**

**Option A: Hybrid model (PREFERRED BY FOUNDER)**
- Require in-person first visit (at clinic/hospital)
- Telemedicine for all follow-ups after initial assessment
- Conservative but bulletproof compliance

**Option B: Partner with physical clinics**
- Hybrid digital-physical model
- Platform coordinates, clinics provide first visit infrastructure

**Option C: Platform handles post-prescription support only**
- Patients get prescription elsewhere
- Come to platform for management, tracking, dietician, community
- Less ambitious but zero regulatory risk

**üö® CRITICAL ACTION ITEM:** Research Telemedicine Practice Guidelines for GLP-1 prescriptions BEFORE building anything. This determines if you need clinic partnerships from day 1. Could fundamentally change MVP scope.
