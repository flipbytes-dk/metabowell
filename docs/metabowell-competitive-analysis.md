## Executive Summary

MetaboWell enters a rapidly growing but fragmented metabolic and hormonal health market in India. The competitive landscape reveals **significant white space** for an integrated, doctor-led, hybrid (physical + digital) platform focused on endocrinology, GLP-1 therapy management, and holistic metabolic care.

### Key Findings

**Market Opportunity:**
- India's weight management market: $3.7B (2024), growing at 9.5% CAGR
- Telemedicine market: $10.6B (2024) ‚Üí $25B (2030) at 20.7% CAGR
- 135M+ Indians living with obesity and metabolic disorders
- **Critical Gap:** No platform integrates endocrinology + GLP-1 + nutrition + digital follow-up comprehensively

**Competitive Dynamics:**
- **Direct Competitors (GLP-1 Programs):** 2 major players (HealthifyRx, Mohak Bariatrics)
- **Indirect Competitors (Weight Management):** 10+ generalist platforms
- **Regional Competition:** Low in Tier-2 cities like Visakhapatnam
- **Pricing Range:** ‚Çπ299-‚Çπ1,500 (consultations) | ‚Çπ2,000-‚Çπ5,000/month (programs) | ‚Çπ48,000-‚Çπ80,000 (GLP-1 therapy)

**Strategic Positioning:**
MetaboWell's **hybrid model** (physical consultations in Vizag + digital follow-up) combined with **medical rigor** (endocrinologist-led) creates a defensible position that pure-play digital platforms and general telemedicine cannot easily replicate.

**Recommendation:** Launch with **Phase 1 focus on Visakhapatnam** to establish regional dominance before national scale, positioning as "India's first integrated metabolic health ecosystem with GLP-1 expertise."
